AncientMetagenomeDir is expanding to now include C14 dating and precise archaeological/historical information for all the samples! Help us in this effort and your future self (no more painful dating information search, all in one place!) 

**Join us in our Hackathon  the 29th of April from 10-17 CEST!**

During the hackathon, we will provide initial training on how to introduce data to the GitHub repository, as well as on interpretation of radiocarbon dates, and work together to introduce data in the AncientMetagenomeDir repository! You can join for as long as you want, all help is welcome!

To join us please register [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdtggNI8yBfxJmO7a9xvi4xAA1xuoQjY8nXVwE2GF9NC-h-NA/viewform)
