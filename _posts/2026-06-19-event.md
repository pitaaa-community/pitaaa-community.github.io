We are excited to announce a new seminar series for the PITAAA isotope community. Attached are posters for talks arranged for the summer and autumn, as well as a poster for our first talk. 

The series aims to create a regular space for researchers from all career stages to share their work, exchange ideas, and discuss the latest developments in isotopic methods in archaeology and its many applications. We hope it will help strengthen connections across our community and encourage new collaborations and discussions.

Each seminar will feature presentations covering a broad range of topics and showcasing both established and emerging research, followed by an opportunity for questions, discussion, and networking.

We would be delighted if you could join us for the first seminar:

"High-resolution 87Sr/86Sr isoscapes and Mobility in Prehistoric Southwest Iberia"
Speaker: Dr Dobereiner Chala Aldana (University of Granada)
Date and Time: Wednesday, July 15 at 4 pm (CEST)
