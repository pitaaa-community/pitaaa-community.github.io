The SPAAM steering committee has organized a speed-networking event between PIs and young researchers (MSc/PhD/Postdocs).


## When is this happening?
Wednesday **April 9th 11.00AM-01.00PM CET**


## What is it?
A virtual event in which young researchers will have the possibility to meet and chat for a limited amount of time with some PIs working in ancient metagenomics and related fields.


## How can I participate?
You can register using the following [google form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1qc7QMZRn-7dAygr1x7IjH1s3JCgJNVTgtIuiyeQdwE8/edit)


List of PIs participating to the event:
Assoc. Prof. Martin Sikora (University of Copenhagen, Globe Institute)
Dr. Lucy van Dorp (University College London, Genetics Institute).
Dr. Alexander Herbig (Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology)
Assoc. Prof. Verena Schuenemann (University of Zurich).
Prof. Ludovic Orlando (Université de Toulouse III, Faculté de Médecine Purpan)
Prof. Nicola Segata (Università Di Trento)
Assoc. Prof. Michael D. Martin (NTNU University Museum)


Please note that assignment to each PI’s slots of time will be done in a first-come first-served way, so, if interested, don't wait to fill out the form!
We will also take into account the expressed preferences when assigning the time slots.


## Want more info?
Check out the [google form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1qc7QMZRn-7dAygr1x7IjH1s3JCgJNVTgtIuiyeQdwE8/edit)
